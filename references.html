Cosmic Tales

Our goal is to bring the science of space weather to life by telling stories from the unique perspectives of different characters, such as a farmer, a photographer, and an astronaut. Through these narratives, we explore how phenomena like solar flares and coronal mass ejections directly impact people's lives and professions on Earth and in space, making complex scientific concepts relatable and engaging for everyone.


Features:

  • Experience stellar stories told from different perspectives
  • Test your knowledge with an interactive quiz
  • Explore a simple and easy-to-understand glossary
  • Access a library of educational resources and references
  • Discover how space weather affects everyday life
